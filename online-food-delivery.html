Key Features of Our Custom Food Delivery App

Escalate your user base with our feature-rich and best in-class online food delivery application. We have a team of dedicated developers that integrates future-ready features in food delivery mobile application, ensuring maximum customer satisfaction to your business by integrating.

PiP (Picture-in-Picture) Mode

Although the feature is limited to Android users only, but looking at the market statistics of Android users, it holds an amazing utility for businesses. The PiP feature enables the users to track the order in real-time while using other apps.

Chat Bot

AI is spreading rapidly in every field of technology. Customer Support is an important aspect of food delivery application which must not be ignored. Integration of ChatBot in the app can solve customer queries right away.

Voice Assistant

The rise of virtual assistants such as Siri, Alexa, and Google assistants have turned the tables of technology. So integrating the feature of voice search in the application will improve the overall customer experience.

Behaviour Tracking

This helps in monitoring the overall user experience of the application by tracking how the users are using the application. In this way the businesses can take critical steps for offering services in a more enhanced way.

Heat Map

Through the data visualization tool, restaurant owners can keep the track about the areas in which food delivery application is getting more user attention. Hence this feature can help businesses to take further important decisions.

Booking Tables

With the food delivery application, the users can easily reserve tables at their favorite restaurants without event going there. This feature enables the users to search for their favorite restaurant and book a table for preferred date and time.
