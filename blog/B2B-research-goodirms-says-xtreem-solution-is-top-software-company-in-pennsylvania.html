B2B Research GoodFirms Says: Xtreem Solution is Top Software Company in Pennsylvania

Written by Xtreem Solution on Feb 28, 2019 1494

Established in 2008, Xtreem Solution stands as a highly competitive mobile application development, website designing, and marketing Company in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The untiring efforts and hard work of the teams at Xtreem have empowered the Company to give tough competition to the top IT and software firms out there.

The Company is true to its tagline ‘PERFECTION DELIVERED’ indeed. If you know us, you know this fact. Read on to know how Xtreem Solution made to the top in the list of Top Software Companies in Pennsylvania.

How Xtreem Solution Creates Start-Up and Enterprise Solution?

Xtreem Solution offers business solutions to both Startups and Big Enterprises for multiple business domains including Health, Real Estate, Education, Beauty and Fashion, Travel, Social Networking, Advertising and Publishing, Events and Tickets. The projects handled by the Company range from CRM Application Development to Custom App Solutions, iPhone to Android Conversion, Android to iPhone Conversion, Online Food Delivery Portal, Online Doctor Appointment Portal and Dating Web Portal Development.

With Xtreem you can expect a website that aligns with the goals and vision of the business and caters to the particular needs of clients. Web development process involves lots of complexities. Handling the different phases from planning to launch is a thing of an expert like Xtreem Solution. The web portals created by them are nothing but a virtual copy of the client ideas and vision.

The Company’s structured approach towards every project starts from understanding the client’s requirements, which is followed by proper documentation of the different phases (detailed below) and forming the road map for an optimized and timely launch of the portal.

The geniuses of Xtreem Solution handle the complexities of every project with ease. The whole procedure is divided into different phases. Here is an insight into their working-  

Understanding the Scope of Project

This phase serves as the foundation of the entire project as it involves understanding and documenting the functional requirements, scope, and objective of the project. All the necessary details are gathered by the concerned staff of the project team. The professionals at Xtreem scrutinize APIs and web services in order to gain a better understanding of third-party systems.  This eliminates the chances of uncertainties during the development phase. Plus, the developers are able to customize the proper Mobile app development solution according to the project’s needs.

It is during this phase of the project that the professionals of Xtreem discover the purpose, target audience and business goals of the business. A thoughtful action plan is created on the basis of these inputs. This plan comprises resource allocation, task breakdown, project milestones, and timeline. The development team also consults finalizes the technologies to be used for CRM application development.

Planning and Drafting Design

In the next phase, the web designing team of Xtreem Solution gathers the guidelines and wireframes necessary for the project. For flawless Ecommerce website development, the design team builds two or more prototypes of the website/application design. The team refines the prototype according to the inputs received from the client.

A high-end Software Development Company like Xtreem knows the importance of attractive site design for achieving increased traffic and better conversion rate. The professionals, for this reason, make sure there is an exchange of ideas between the clients, designers, and coders and clients. It allows the developers to build a website matching the specific needs of the client.

Building the Important Elements for Development Phase

In this phase the developers’ focus on developing the functional elements of the website. The experienced programmers of Xtreem Solution customize the functionalities and features finalized in the first stage of the project development. There is a lot that goes into Mobile app development solution offered by the Company. This involves the integration of backend database with the user interface, coding, and much more.

The Xtreem Solution staff responsible for handling the development phase also deal with installation, configuration, and customization of databases, frameworks and Content management systems. Necessary steps are taken to ensure scalable and standardized CRM application development.

Quality Check Phase

In the next step, the professionals of this software development company make check the quality standards of the web application. Every customized feature goes through a comprehensive quality check process. The various quality assurance tests include regression testing, browser responsive testing, load performance testing, functional testing, etc.

It is this phase where the eCommerce website development team works closely with the coding team and the client. The whole point is to assure a bug-free and smooth end product. Towards the end of this phase, Xtreem Solution sends the product for client review.

Real-Time Testing of the Product

Xtreem Solution allows the client or end user to review the app/site in order to get the suggestions or real-time feedback on the product. It is crucial to know about views of the concerned party so that the Mobile app development solution can be further modified (if needed). A pilot test on the newly built website application allows the developers to make it full proof in every aspect. Once the full review of the client is received, requested changes are made in the website/application. Post client approval the site is prepared for launch.

Preparing the Product for Launch

In the final phase of CRM application development, the developers roll into production and launch the product for the target audience. A lot of preparation goes into this final step. The experts at Xtreem Solution leave no stone unturned to meet the expectations of clients when it comes to delivering the final work.

Redirection of the domain name, sanity testing, data migration, code merge, and server configuration are some of the important tasks that are performed by the team collectively in this phase. A team of experienced professionals carries these tasks with great care and utmost precision. They ensure everything is set up correctly. Xtreem Solution also facilitates its clients with ongoing maintenance, periodic updates, and support when needed.

Company Overview

Popular as optimal digital solution providers, Xtreem holds extensive expertise and vast experience in Mobile App and web Development. The Company offers Start-Up Solution and Enterprise Solution as well. Their quality-centered services are customized after in-depth analysis and proper understanding of the clients’ requirements. The professionals of Xtreem maintain professional work ethics and dedicated approach to all their projects, irrespective of the project’s size and complexity.

Housing the most qualified and creative professionals of the industry, Xtreem is known for its highly standardized, time-honored digital solutions. Their credibility is unquestionable. The team leverages advanced technology and proven methodology to garner the attention of the perspective. This software development company engages with the aims and vision of clients to ensure precise and profound end result.

The futuristic solutions developed by them are formulated according to the international coding standards. It is all because of the firm commitment and diligence of the Company’s management and staff that the Company ranks among Fortune 500 clients. Having served 490+ satisfied clients the tech firm spreads its roots to 13+ countries. It boasts 4500+ completed projects to its credit.

In a nutshell, Xtreem Solution has truly earned the dignified rank. The proven and well-structured web development processes followed by the Company stand as the testimony of their competency in eCommerce website development.
