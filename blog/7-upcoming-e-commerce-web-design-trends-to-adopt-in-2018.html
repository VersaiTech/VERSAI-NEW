7 Upcoming E-commerce Web Design Trends to Adopt in 2018

Written by Vikash Jangid on May 27, 2017 772

With the advancement in the technology, most of the individuals have just forgotten the time when they used to buy the things manually. Now the businesses are coming online with the e-commerce website. In this era of modernity, most of the individuals do not have much time to go to the shop and buy the things they need. Due to this, there is a rise in online shopping day by day from the e-commerce website. As there is the vying number of websites on the internet, thus the people go to the websites which are highly captivating and easy to be navigated.  This means that the websites need to be highly adaptable and responsive to all the mobile screens as the people use their mobile phones for shopping online.
Nowadays the big players in the online shopping site are Amazon and Flipkart which are leading the impact by presenting new innovations. To get ahead in the competition of the e-commerce business, the businesses nowadays need to discover what is new in the market and they can impeccably make the impression on the users. To remain in the industry the E-commerce website development company needs to be updated with the current trends.
Some upcoming web design trends to be adopted in 2018:
 Mobile E-commerce taking place:
 According to the latest research e-commerce is the second name of mobile shopping. The shopping through the mobile has broken all the records of shopping as compared to the previous shopping being done through the previous way. The mobile shopping has increased up to the sales of all the e-commerce businesses to a great extent. As per the research stats, the shopping through the mobile is rising rapidly. The customers love to search the things with the use of mobile rather than the laptops or mobiles. They love to explore different kinds of things in the e-commerce stores. Thus, all the web owners need to adopt this rising and the latest trend in e-commerce by hiring best e-commerce website development company.
 Payment through mobile will be rise:
If you are a lover of online shopping through mobile, then you are sure about the payment through the mobile. Thus, you want the smooth payment through the payment gateways. So, the website owners need to offer the smooth payment gateways which makes the user to visit again and again on the website of e-commerce. So, just creating the mobile-friendly e-commerce website is not just enough to attract the users, if you are highly serious for your business then you need to also take care about the payment gateway feature on your website. This will enable the user to make the payments simply with their devices.
Efficient Loading and layout will be raised:
Mobile data will become the major concern of the individuals. Moreover, the packages being offered by various service providers are becoming generous. So, the E-commerce website development company need to look at the concern of the efficient loading of the website such that the user did not need to to use their data much in just loading the website.
Instead of listing 100 product on one page just load 10 products on a single page and then just scroll the list at the bottom of the existing pages. The e-commerce websites which loads efficiently are loaded much as a comparison to another kind of websites as the individuals will be data hungry always.

Hamburgers on left will become more trendy:
From years the hamburger menus are in trend and they will continue in the future too. Might be you have seen many sites which are using the hamburger menus. For this, the framework which is used by the designers widely is the bootstrap. The sites which are developed with the use of the bootstrap framework. This pattern is followed by the designers all over the world. But in 2018, you will see the little change in the hamburger menu i.e. you will now check the menus on the left. As most of, the sites have started placing their menus on the left such as the google.
Use of the Bright Colors More:
This is one of the most vital design trends which need to keep in the mind while the development of the website in 2018. As you can expect the most explosion of bright colors on the e-commerce website. This is just done to catch the attention of the users through the demographics done on the website. Thus, E-commerce development services company needs to prepare themselves and brace themselves with the upcoming trend of bright colors in the websites.
Motion Animation:
This will become the most current and cool trend of the e-commerce development industry. This will become the excellent and the nonstandard method for just showing the item with the utilization of the animation that gives user good experience using the e-commerce website. To give user good shopping experience this needs to be indulged in the upcoming design trend by the industry. For the businesses which are highly serious about their sales perspective, they need to offer the user with the great shopping experience.
Voice Search will take Place:
In the coming year 2018, in the e-commerce industry voice search will take place and this has been adopted by most of the e-commerce websites now only. Integrating this feature, will grab the attention of the user s and increase the traffic of the e-commerce website. Thus, this feature in the web design industry will take place for the shopping.

 Fast Delivery with Free Shipping:
Despite offering the flexible payment flexibility to the user by the e-commerce website. In the upcoming year, the website users will also improve their shipping features such that the users can avail their products in the time they need. They will offer the special delivery too of just 24 hours on the doorstep of the user. This will surely be the cool upcoming web design trend.
Conclusion:
Going through the blog you will get to know that having the website of e-commerce is easy, but to face the competition it really much important for the owners to innovate their website with the upcoming trends. So, the e-commerce development service company always needs to be updated so that they can offer their client with the best website.
